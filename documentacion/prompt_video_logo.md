1. Sube tu logo (`Gemini_Generated_Image_4lymra4lymra4lym.png`).
2. Configura el logo como **Imagen Inicial** e **Imagen Final**.
3. Pega el siguiente prompt:

**Prompt:**

A dense swirling white smoke gradually parts from the center in a fluid, organic motion to reveal the minimalist logo. Slow and steady cinematic dolly-in zoom. Elegant glowing particles and micro-sparks floating in the air catching the light. Soft volumetric lighting with a subtle glow around the logo. High contrast, professional 4k motion graphics, mysterious and premium mood. Using provided initial and final frames for consistency."
